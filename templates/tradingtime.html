THIS BLOG IS TO DESCRIBE THE TIMINGS OF INDIAN STOCK MARKET.



Indian Stock Market is open from Monday to Friday.It is closed on weekends i.e, Saturdays and Sundays.
And if there are any national holidays then they are predeclared and stock markets remain closed on those days.

Indian Stock Market Time takes place in 3 session:


1.Pre Open Session which is open for 15 minutes(9:00am to 9:15am) which is again divided into 3 session.


a)Ordinary Entry Session(9:am to 9:08am).
b)Order Matching Session(9:08am to 9:12am).
c)Buffer Session(9:12am to 9:15am).
IMPORTANT: Traders can buy or sell in pre-open session.

2.Normal Market Session


Normal Market Session is regularly trading from 9:15am to 3:30pm.It's actual session in which most of the trades takes place.

3.Post Closing Session


It's start from 3:40pm and ends at 4:00pm.During this session buying and selling shares take place at closing price.Normal traders could not trade at this time.
